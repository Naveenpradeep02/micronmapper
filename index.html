What is Photogrammetry?

Photogrammetry is a revolutionary imaging technique that captures ultra-precise 3D positions of dental implants in the oral cavity. Originally used in fields like engineering and aerospace, this technology has now transformed digital dentistry by allowing dentists to create highly accurate dental prosthetics with unmatched speed and precision.
